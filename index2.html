History of the Bengals

In 1967, Paul Brown (pictured below, left), considered to be one of the NFL's greatest coaches, was granted the opportunity to start his very own NFL football team. In 1968 the Bengals were born. Since then, the bengals have won the AFC championship twice in the 80's and a third time this past year in 2022 (pictured below, right). To commemorate the late Paul Browns amazing work with the team, the Bengals moved to Paul Brown stadium in 2001.
